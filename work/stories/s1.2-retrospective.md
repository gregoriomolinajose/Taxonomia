# Retrospective: s1.2 App Bootstrap (Global Prefetch)

## What went well
- The `getAppBootstrapPayload()` endpoint was successfully implemented.
- We strictly followed the architectural guidelines to map tuples to arrays of objects on the backend to preserve frontend stability.
- Zero-latency transitions were achieved.

## What could be improved
- We missed the centralized `Global_Config.js` override initially, forcing a second deploy. Future deployments should ensure environment configs are bumped properly.

## Action Items
- Always bump versions in `environments/Config.dev.js` and `environments/Config.prod.js` before deploying.
