## User Story
As a Product Owner,
I want a full schema audit and a graceful fallback mechanism in FormEngine UI,
So that older entity schemas (like Portafolios, Unidades de Negocio) don't cause a White Screen of Death due to missing `section` and `width` properties, maintaining backward compatibility while we refactor towards Blueprint V3.

## Acceptance Criteria
- All schemas in APP_SCHEMAS (`Schema_Engine.gs`) have `width` and `section` attributes.
- The `primaryKey` property aligns with the ID field in schemas.
- `FormEngine_UI.html` provides a graceful fallback for missing `width` and `section` properties, and emits a `console.warn` upon missing schema properties.
- All functions defined in `lookupSource` within schemas exist in the backend (`API_Universal.gs` or `Cache_Utils.gs`) and correctly return `[{id, nombre}]`.
- The Build Tracker is updated to `v1.0.30`.
- An Audit Report is delivered.
