## User Story

**As a** System Architect
**I want** to correct the base topology in Taxonomia Schema for Equipo and Persona
**So that** the backend correctly persists the PERSONA_EQUIPO SCD-2 edges and the UI can access real relationships instead of fake flat data.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `Persona.equipo` is correctly defined as a relation type pointing to `Equipo`.
2. `Equipo` relationship to product is refactored: It now points to `Grupo_Productos` (relationType: "padre") to reflect that teams govern a business stream, not an individual product.
3. `Equipo` has a new child relation subgrid for `Personas`.
4. Typo `"Equipas"` is corrected to `"Equipo"` in all lookup descriptors.
5. All relationships related to this use `isTemporalGraph: true` and define `graphEdgeType`. Personas recursive org-hierarchy (`lider_directo`) is also verified and tagged correctly to separate it from the agile schema.
6. Unit tests in `Equipo_CRUD_Fix.test.js` are un-skipped and updated to match the new schema behavior.
