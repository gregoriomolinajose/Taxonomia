## Heutagogical Checkpoint

### 1. What did you learn?
Learned the impact of sandbox/iframe environments in Google Apps Script on F12 console debugging. Also identified that passing invalid collections (objects vs arrays) to factory builders is a common source of White Screen of Death (WSOD) in this architecture.

### 2. What would you change about the process?
Implement a "Sanity Probe" in the test suite that verifies the presence of all required architectural registries (`UI_Factory`, `BuilderRegistry`) before executing complex specs.

### 3. Are there improvements for the framework?
Strengthen the input validation in `UI_Factory`. Components should fail gracefully or default to stable states when receiving malformed datasets instead of throwing `TypeError: .find is not a function`.

### 4. What are you more capable of now?
Designing "Deep Integration Tests" that audit specific memory-safety concerns (H10) without relying on external heavy testing frameworks, keeping the PWA light and diagnosable.
